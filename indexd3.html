Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brise

Wow you taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teapoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teapoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more fot the pan

For the Topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoor olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not
    necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh frozen cranberries

Method

1.Make the focaccia dough
